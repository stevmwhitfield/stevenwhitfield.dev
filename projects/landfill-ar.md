## Project Overview

As part of Florida Poly's Senior Design course, myself and three other students formed a team to
build an AR mobile app for the local landfill. It serves to educate users on the landfill and how it
effects the the environment as well as visualize the phases throughout its lifecycle.

## Technologies Used

- [Unity](https://unity.com/)
- [Microsoft Azure and Speech SDK](https://speech.microsoft.com/portal)
- [Google Translate API](https://cloud.google.com/translate/docs/reference/rest/)

## What I Learned

This project gave me a lot of new experiences. Working in a structured team, using unfamiliar APIs
and cloud services, and presenting our work to a public organization to name a few.

All school projects before this were designed to serve a fictional client, if one at all. With this
project, our team worked with a real client with real needs. We met several times with several
personnel to better understand their needs and discuss the direction of the project.

As for the app itself, I built a text-to-speech feature and helped with a dynamic translation
feature that used Azure's speech service and the Google Translate API, respectively. I had not used
either before this.

## Future Plans

At the end of the course, the project was handed over to the landfill to use however they see fit.
This team's role in the project is over. Should the landfill choose to continue development of the
app, it will be designated to a new team of students.
