## Project Overview

I worked with a small team to make a proof-of-concept for an e-commerce shop as part of Florida
Poly's Software Engineering course.

We used the MERN stack - MongoDB, Express, React, and Node.js - to build our solution.

## Technologies Used

- [React](https://react.dev/)
- [Node.js](https://nodejs.org/)
- [Express](http://expressjs.com/)
- [MongoDB](https://www.mongodb.com/)

## What I Learned

Communication is key when working with a team. Our backend developer made a mistake in his time
management and we underestimated just how much this would impact the project timeline. At the rate
we were going, the project wasn't going to meet the deadline. Since I had completed the frontend by
that point and there was only a few days left, the team lead gave me the responsibility of
developing the backend.

Now, my only experience with Node.js and Express at this point was spinning up a server and
displaying "Hello World" on the index page. I researched the technologies and scraped together a
backend that had models, API routes, and used Mongoose to interact with our NoSQL MongoDB Atlas
database. It wasn't pretty, but it worked.
